My Hobby

Here are some of my favorite hobbies and interests.

Playing Story-Based Games

I have a deep passion for story-based games and love to immerse myself in their rich narratives and character-driven plots. For me, gaming is a form of interactive storytelling, offering unique emotional journeys and a chance to explore vast, imaginative worlds.

Game Content Creator

Beyond just playing, I take my passion a step further by creating and sharing gaming content. This involves editing gameplay videos, make a guide about the game for public, and engaging with a like-minded community, allowing me to blend my creative and technical skills at Tiktok platform.

Watching Anime

My interest in visual arts extends to the vibrant world of Japanese animation. I am constantly amazed by the masterful storytelling, unique art styles, and diverse genres that anime offers, from epic adventures to thought-provoking dramas.
